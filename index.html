Workshop Goals

We are pleased to announce the MBZUAI-Berkeley Joint Workshop on Emerging Directions in Machine Learning, organized by the Machine Learning Department at MBZUAI. This workshop aims to foster collaboration and the exchange of ideas on the latest advancements in machine learning. Bringing together leading experts from UC Berkeley and MBZUAI faculty, the event will explore cutting-edge techniques, share groundbreaking research, and identify key challenges and opportunities shaping the future of machine learning theory and applications.

Schedule

We will host 1 day of invited talks (March 12) interspersed with panel discussions on emerging directions of machine learning.
